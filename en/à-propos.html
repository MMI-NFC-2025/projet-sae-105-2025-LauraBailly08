About me

MMI student

Group TPB1

I was responsible for documentary research and writing the articles on Lise Meitner and the Matilda effect as part of an educational exercise in the MMI department of Montbéliard.

My approach

This project was born out of a desire to shed light on a historical injustice too often overlooked: the Matilda effect, a systematic phenomenon that erases the contributions of women scientists.

By focusing on Lise Meitner, a brilliant physicist whose role in the discovery of nuclear fission was minimized, I wanted to create an accessible site that raises awareness among the general public.

My approach combines rigorous documentation (academic sources, archives, scientific references) and pedagogy (popularization, visual content, cultural references).
